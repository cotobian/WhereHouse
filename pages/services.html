CFS Warehouse
Our CFS (Container Freight Station) service provides an optimal solution for cargo management, helping customers save time and costs during transportation and storage processes. At Wherehouse Technologies Co., Ltd., we understand that each shipment has its own value and specific requirements, so our service is designed to flexibly meet the unique needs of each customer. Key features of our CFS warehousing service include:
  • Spacious Storage Capacity: With modern, expansive facilities, we are equipped to receive and manage goods on a large scale, ensuring that cargo is stored securely and is easily accessible.
  • Smart Goods Management: Our warehouse management system utilizes advanced technology, allowing for efficient tracking and control of goods, minimizing the risk of loss or damage.
  • Professional Packing Service: Our experienced team will assist in packing and preparing goods before shipment, ensuring that cargo is well-protected throughout the transportation process.
Customs Declaration
WHEREHOUSE TECHNOLOGIES is proud to offer professional, quick, and efficient customs declaration services, helping businesses easily carry out import and export procedures smoothly. With a team of experienced professionals who are knowledgeable about customs regulations, we are committed to providing optimal solutions that minimize risks and ensure accuracy in every process. Our customs declaration services include:
  • Customs Consulting: We assist businesses in understanding regulations, policies, and selecting the appropriate HS codes.
  • Preparation of Customs Documentation: We ensure all necessary paperwork is completed according to the requirements of the authorities.
  • Customs Declaration and Clearance: We represent businesses in filing declarations and coordinating with customs authorities to expedite the clearance process.
  • Issue Resolution: We assist in addressing any arising situations and provide advice on handling specific issues related to each type of cargo.
With advanced technology and optimized processes, WHEREHOUSE TECHNOLOGIES is ready to accompany businesses on their journey of integration, market expansion, and sustainable business development.
